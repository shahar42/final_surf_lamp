Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2025

What We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with surf condition monitoring:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password (encrypted)
  • Device Information: Arduino device ID linked to your account
  • Preferences: Location, sport type, wave/wind thresholds, and display settings
  • Usage Data: Lamp status and surf condition requests

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide real-time surf and wind conditions to your lamp
  • Authenticate your account and manage your preferences
  • Send weather alerts based on your configured thresholds
  • Improve system performance and troubleshoot issues

Data Security

Your data is protected with industry-standard security measures:

  • Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing
  • All connections use HTTPS encryption
  • Database access is restricted and monitored
  • We never sell or share your personal information with third parties

Third-Party Services

We use weather data from OpenWeatherMap and Open-Meteo to provide surf conditions. These services do not receive your personal information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time via your dashboard
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data upon request

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please use the "Report Error" button on your dashboard to contact us.
