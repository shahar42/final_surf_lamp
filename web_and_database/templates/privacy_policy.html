Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

Effective Date: December 29, 2025

TL;DR: We collect your email, location (surf spot), and lamp preferences to make your Surf Lamp work. We use bcrypt encryption, don't sell your data, and you can delete your account anytime.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Data: Username, email address, encrypted password
  • Location Data: Surf spot/beach location (e.g., "Ashkelon Marina")
  • Preferences: Wave height threshold, wind speed threshold, sport type (surfing/kitesurfing), lamp theme, brightness level, off-times schedule
  • Hardware ID: Arduino device ID linked to your lamp
  • Error Reports: If you submit error reports, we collect your description, browser type, and timestamp

Surf Condition Data

  • Weather Data: Wave height, wave period, wind speed, wind direction (fetched from public weather APIs for your location)
  • Last Update Timestamps: When surf conditions were last refreshed

WiFi Network Data (Stored on Your Lamp, Not Our Servers)

  • Your WiFi SSID and password are stored ONLY on your ESP32 lamp device in encrypted non-volatile storage
  • Nearby WiFi network names (SSIDs) are stored on your lamp to detect location changes
  • Important: This data never leaves your lamp and is NOT sent to our servers

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Fetch surf conditions for your location and send them to your lamp
  • Personalization: Apply your threshold alerts, theme, brightness, and off-times schedule
  • Account Management: Authenticate logins, process password resets, link your lamp to your account
  • Support: Respond to error reports and troubleshoot technical issues
  • Improvement: Analyze error patterns to fix bugs and improve the service

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL) hosted in the EU with encryption at rest
  • Web Service: Render.com servers with HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (industry-standard one-way encryption)
  • Access Control: Only authorized system administrators can access the database

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: Supabase (database), Render.com (hosting), weather APIs (Open-Meteo, OpenWeatherMap) - only necessary data for service operation
  • Legal Requirements: If required by Israeli law or court order

5. Your Rights (Israeli Privacy Protection Law)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update incorrect information via your dashboard
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data at any time (use "Delete Account" button on dashboard)
  • Portability: Request your data in machine-readable format (JSON)
  • Object: Opt out of data processing (note: this will prevent your lamp from working)

To exercise these rights, contact: [YOUR EMAIL HERE]

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: All personal data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Error Reports: Retained for 90 days for troubleshooting, then deleted
  • Password Reset Tokens: Expire after 1 hour, deleted after 24 hours

7. Cookies & Tracking

  • Session Cookies: Used to keep you logged in (deleted when you log out)
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do NOT use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising trackers

8. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that poses significant risk to your privacy, we will:

  • Notify the Israeli Privacy Protection Authority (PPA) within 72 hours
  • Notify affected users via email within 72 hours
  • Provide details of the breach, affected data, and steps to protect yourself

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on EU servers (Supabase) and may be processed by US-based services (Render.com). All transfers comply with GDPR adequacy requirements.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect legal changes or service improvements. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the service after updates constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact Information

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns:

  • Email: [YOUR EMAIL HERE]
  • Israeli Privacy Protection Authority: www.gov.il/privacy

This privacy policy complies with the Israeli Privacy Protection Law, 1981 (as amended 2024).
